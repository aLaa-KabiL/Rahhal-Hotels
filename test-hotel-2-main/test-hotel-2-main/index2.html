Hotel Details

The Empire Hotel New York

Price Per Night : $ 1150

"Why is the Empire Hotel one , of the best hotels in New York!"

This Manhattan hotel is 272 feet from the Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Central Park is 528 feet from the The Empire Hotel New York, It features a seasonal rooftop outdoor pool. The modern rooms at the The Empire Hotel New York are decorated in earthy tones and equipped with a flat-screen TV and an iPod docking station, Guests will find a rain shower head in the bathroom. The seasonal rooftop plunge pool is surrounded by a sun deck with large lounge chairs providing panoramic views of New York City, Food and drink at the Pool Deck is also available.

Room 1: Accessible Room King Bed

- Stylishly designed Superior Room with 1 King Bed with ADA Accessible features such as a Roll-in Shower with Grab Bars, lowered sinks, and more

Room 2: Deluxe Room King Bed

- Opulent comfort with stunning Lincoln Center views. A generous space featuring 1 King Bed, 50" HDTV, and chic decor, blending luxury with artistic flair.

Room 3: Junior Room King Bed

- Stunning views of Lincoln Center or Dante Park await you in our Junior Room with 1 King Bed. Featuring a separate sitting area with a pull-out sofa, perfect for an unforgettable city escape.

Room 4: Superior Room King Bed

- Revel in the blend of vintage glamour and modern luxuries in our Superior King room. Offering serene courtyard & Upper West side views, with 1 King sized bed.

Room 5: Standard Room Queen Bed

- Designed with Mid-Century Modern flair, Standard Rooms provide an intimate urban retreat with 1 Queen sized bed. Immerse yourself in art deco elegance & modern comforts.

Room 6: Superior Room Double Beds

- Ideal for travel with family & friends, with 2 Double Beds. Unwind with plush bedding, ensuring comfort and style for up to four guests.
